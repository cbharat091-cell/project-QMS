# Sample QMS Procedure

## Document Control
- Title: Quality Management System Procedure
- Version: 1.0
- Effective Date: 2024-01-01
- Document Owner: Quality Manager
- Approved By: CEO

## Purpose and Scope
This procedure establishes the quality management system for our organization. It applies to all departments and processes within the company.

## Roles and Responsibilities
The Quality Manager is responsible for implementing and maintaining the QMS. Each department head is accountable for compliance within their areas.

## Process Flow
1. Identify process requirements
2. Document procedures
3. Implement controls
4. Monitor and measure outcomes
5. Continually improve

## Risk Management
We identify risks and hazards throughout our processes. Severity and probability are assessed for each identified risk. Mitigation strategies are developed and implemented.

## CAPA and Nonconformance
When nonconformances are identified, corrective action is taken to address root cause. Preventive actions are implemented to avoid recurrence. All CAPA activities are documented and tracked.

## Records and Retention
All quality records are maintained and retained according to our retention schedule. Documented information is archived for traceability purposes.
